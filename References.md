# References

This repository accompanies the poster

# **Invisible Higgs Boson Decay in a Diracon Model**

The bibliography below contains the principal references used throughout this work. These include the theoretical framework of Dirac neutrino mass generation, the Type-I seesaw mechanism, invisible Higgs decays, lepton flavour violation, and the experimental inputs employed in the phenomenological analysis.

---

## Diracon Models

### Flavour and cosmological probes of Diracon models

**S. Centelles Chuliá, T. Herbermann, A. Herrero-Brocal, and A. Vicente**

*Journal of High Energy Physics* **09** (2025), 110.

DOI: https://doi.org/10.1007/JHEP09(2025)110

This work provides the theoretical framework of the Diracon model analyzed in the poster, including its scalar sector, neutrino mass generation, flavour observables, and cosmological implications.

---

## Type-I Seesaw Mechanism

### The Type-I Seesaw family

**S. Centelles Chuliá, A. Herrero-Brocal, and A. Vicente**

*Journal of High Energy Physics* **07** (2024), 060.

DOI: https://doi.org/10.1007/JHEP07(2024)060

A modern review of Type-I seesaw models and their theoretical structure.

### The seesaw mechanism at arbitrary order: disentangling the small scale from the large scale

**W. Grimus and L. Lavoura**

*Journal of High Energy Physics* **11** (2000), 042.

DOI: https://doi.org/10.1088/1126-6708/2000/11/042

Classical reference on the perturbative diagonalization of seesaw mass matrices.
